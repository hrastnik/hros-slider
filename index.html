Usage

Include script on your page.

    <script src="hros-slider.min.js"></script>

Initialize slider when your content is loaded.

    <script>
        new hros.Slider(); 
    </script>

Wrap everything in .hros-slider. Add .hros-slider-goto to goto buttons and give them 'data-goto' attribute with index of the slide they go to. Add .hros-slider-slide to slides.
